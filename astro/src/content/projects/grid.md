In August 2025, I was approached by a friend who had an idea to for a platform to transform the NIL (Name, Image, Likeness) space in college sports. After hearing his vision, I decided to join him as a founding software engineer to turn his vision into a reality.

Since then, we have been hard at work building the platform, which we have named GRID. GRID connects college athletes with brands and financial advisors, allowing them to get the most of their NIL deals.

We are actively working on the platform and have a lot of exciting features that we're working on. If you're interested in learning more about GRID or want to get involved, please reach out!

For more information, visit our website: [gridnil.com](https://gridnil.com).
